## Finding: Champion Ability Score Is Root Cause

MAX_TURNS = 30. Avg turns 29.6-29.7 for Elf/Demon matchups = hitting turn limit constantly.

Score hierarchy: lethal(1000) > winning attack(50+) > **championAbility(20)** > advance(15) > buff(15) > 5-cost summon(15) > 4-cost summon(12) > throne approach(12)

Champion ability scoring 20 beats ALL non-attack actions. AI spends 2 mana/turn on ability before developing board. Less board pressure → games timeout → draws.

Before Fix 1 (no abilities): 38% draw.  
After Fix 1 (ability=20): 81% draw.
