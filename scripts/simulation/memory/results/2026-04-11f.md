## Root Cause: No Closing Instinct

Elf/Demon matchups avg 29.5-29.9 turns = hitting 30-turn limit constantly.
Attack on enemy champion scores `dmgToChamp * 10` regardless of champion HP.
A 2-ATK attack on a 5-HP champion (near-lethal!) scores only 20 — same as buff spells.
The AI has no urgency to finish wounded champions before the timer expires.

Beast closes games because Rush units attack immediately after summoning.
Non-Rush factions can't sustain enough pressure to close within 30 turns.

## Proposed Fix 5 (awaiting approval)
Champion-HP-aware urgency scaling:
- Attack on champion: dmgToChamp * 20 (HP≤8), * 15 (HP≤15), * 10 (otherwise)
- Advance toward champion: 35 (HP≤8), 22 (HP≤15), 15 (otherwise)
