## KEY FINDING: Healing Is NOT the Primary Draw Cause

Bloom win rate when PLAYED: 7.5%
Bloom win rate when NOT DRAWN: 8.7%

Nearly identical — healing removal would not meaningfully change the draw rate.
Even without Bloom, Human only beats Elf 8.7% of the time.

The REAL issue: greedy heuristic AI cannot coordinate multi-turn attacks to
concentrate damage on the enemy champion. No forward planning = never achieve
simultaneous 3-unit champion strikes that would deal 9-12 HP in one turn.
